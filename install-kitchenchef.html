How to Install on Your Phone

Kitchen Chef is a web app that installs directly from your browser. There is no app store download required. It works offline once installed.

Android (Chrome)

  1. Tap the link above to open Kitchen Chef in Chrome.
  2. If Chrome shows an "Install" or "Add to Home Screen" banner at the bottom, tap it.
  3. If no banner appears: tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right corner, then tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install app."
  4. Tap "Install" to confirm.
  5. The app icon will appear on your home screen. Tap it to open the app anytime.

iPhone / iPad (Safari)

  1. Open the link above in Safari (not Chrome or another browser).
  2. Tap the Share button (the square with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen."
  4. Tap "Add" in the top-right corner.
  5. The app icon will appear on your home screen. Tap it to open the app anytime.

Desktop (Windows, Mac, Chromebook)

  1. Open the link above in Chrome, Edge, or another modern browser.
  2. Look for an install icon in the address bar (a monitor with a down arrow, or a "+" icon).
  3. Click it and confirm the installation.
  4. The app will appear in your system's app launcher or desktop.

How to Completely Uninstall

Android

  1. Long-press the app icon on your home screen.
  2. Tap "Uninstall" or drag it to the trash icon.
  3. To remove all stored data: open Chrome, go to Settings → Site Settings → All Sites, find abmeapps.github.io, and tap "Clear & Reset."

iPhone / iPad

  1. Long-press the app icon on your home screen.
  2. Tap "Remove Bookmark" or "Delete."
  3. To remove all stored data: open Safari, go to Settings → Safari → Advanced → Website Data, find abmeapps.github.io, and swipe to delete.

Desktop

  1. Open the app, click the three-dot menu in the title bar, and select "Uninstall."
  2. Check "Also clear data" if offered, then confirm.
Your data stays on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all data stored by the app. If you want to keep a copy, use the app's export feature before uninstalling.
