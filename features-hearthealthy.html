Healthy Heart v0.9.0

Cardiac Health Tracker
  • Every BP reading instantly classified by AHA standards with color coding.
  • Tracks blood pressure, pulse, oxygen saturation, weight, symptoms, and body position.
  • One-tap medication check-off with adherence history. Reports show which meds were taken and when.
  • Bluetooth reads directly from a BP cuff — no manual entry, no transcription errors.
  • Visual trend charts show whether a treatment plan is working over weeks.
  • An optional AI Help Assistant answers questions inside the app. An interpretation engine flags patterns for providers.

Patients arrive at appointments with a professional clinical report instead of handwritten notes.

Try Healthy Heart
Healthy Heart is a prototype under active development. It works on any phone, tablet, or PC. All data stays on your device — nothing is sent to a server. Install it directly from your browser with no app store required.
